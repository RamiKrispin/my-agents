# Voice & Structure Guide — `default`

A neutral instructional voice for when no specialized profile fits. Clear,
friendly, and concrete. For principle/workflow workshops, prefer the
`design-principles` profile instead.

## Voice

- Clear and direct; second person ("you") is fine here.
- Define terms on first use. Short paragraphs, one idea per sentence.
- Show, don't just tell — pair concepts with a concrete example.
- Plain language over jargon; no hype adjectives.

## Slide section structure (per topic)

- Section divider with the canonical topic title `{NN} — {Topic Title}`.
- Open with a brief recap of the previous topic; close the section with a
  forward bridge to the next.
- Slides are quiet beside the spoken delivery — keep them sparse.
- Workshops have **no scripts**; the slides plus topic notes are the artifacts.

## Topic README (only when the spec opts in)

- H1 `# {NN} — {Topic Title}`; opens with the topic goal.
- `##` sections, code/Mermaid where useful, short "Recap & next".

## Avoid

- Hype adjectives, inconsistent terminology, and unverified technical claims.

The slide look for this profile is in `slide-style.md` (the base deck theme).
